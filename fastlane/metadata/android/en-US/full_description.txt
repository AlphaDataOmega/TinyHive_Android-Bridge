TinyHive Bridge connects your Android phone to your personal TinyHive AI assistant, enabling hands-free control of your apps.

🔗 SEAMLESS CONNECTION
• Scan a QR code or tap a link to pair instantly
• Secure WebSocket connection to YOUR hive only
• Auto-reconnects when connection drops

🎯 APP CONTROL
• Open any app by name
• Tap buttons and UI elements
• Swipe, scroll, and navigate
• Type text into any input field
• Read screen content

🔒 PRIVACY FIRST
• Connects only to YOUR personal hive
• No data sent to third parties
• All commands encrypted in transit
• You control what actions are allowed

⚡ ALWAYS READY
• Runs as a background service
• Starts automatically on boot
• Low battery impact

REQUIREMENTS
• Android 8.0 or higher
• A running TinyHive instance
• Accessibility Service permission (required for app control)

HOW IT WORKS
1. Install the app
2. Enable Accessibility Service in Settings
3. Scan the QR code from your TinyHive dashboard
4. Done! Your hive can now control apps on this device

PERFECT FOR
• Hands-free phone control
• Automation workflows
• Accessibility assistance
• Remote device management
